Privacy Policy

At AutoCare, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you use our services, such as name, contact details, vehicle information, and payment information. This data is collected when you register, schedule an appointment, or interact with us through our website or mobile app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the best service possible, including processing service appointments, sending reminders, managing payment details, and offering personalized experiences. We may also use your data to improve our services and respond to your inquiries.

3. How We Protect Your Information

AutoCare takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement a variety of security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to service providers who assist us in providing services to you.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our support team.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@autocare.com.
